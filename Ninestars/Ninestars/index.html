Challenge your friends in this popular game of location guessing!

KeSi is a fun social media game-like app where you and your friends do your best to guess eachother's locations.

Get Started

Challenge and get challenged in location guessing!

Introducing KeSi, your best adventure buddy! KeSi? is your go-to for transforming mundane moments into thrilling guessing games with friends.

Picture this: whether you're exploring new territories or stumbling upon places you rarely visit, KeSi is there, ready to spice things up. It's simple: when the moment strikes, KeSi prompts you to snap three photos of your surroundings. Then, the excitement begins!

Your friends become detectives, using only the first photo you share to pinpoint your exact location on Google Maps. The closer their guess, the more points they rack up. But here's the catch: peeking at more than one photo deducts points, intensifying the challenge and thrill. The best guessers secure their spot on the prestigious leaderboard, earning well-deserved bragging rights.

You can also join the fun by guessing your friends' locations and posting your own guesses. With KeSi, every corner of the world becomes an exciting guessing game!
